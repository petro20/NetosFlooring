Our story 20+ years across New Jersey

Some things are built to last, and so are the people behind them.

C & A Home Improvement started over 20 years ago with hardwood flooring specialists and a craftsman who believed that how you treat someone's home says everything about who you are.

We started as hardwood flooring specialists, because of our attention to detail that carries into everything we do. Today we proudly handle kitchens, bathrooms, stairs, vinyl, and full renovations across New Jersey, with the same hands-on care that built our name one floor at a time.

Whether you're refreshing a single room or remodeling your entire home, you'll work directly with our crew, experienced craftsmen who treat your home the way they'd treat their own.

We believe the best renovation experience is built on three things: honest communication, clean workmanship, and showing up when we say we will. That's how we've earned the trust of homeowners across New Jersey for more than two decades.

Whether the job is handled by our crew or a trusted specialist, C & A oversees every detail and we're always your single point of contact.

What do our clients notice? The details. The ones most contractors overlook. The clean job site at the end of every day. The call you actually get back. The finished floor that looks even better than the sample. The kitchen that finally works the way your family lives.

That's not an accident. That's 20 years of showing up and giving a damn.
