Our Services

For over 20 years, C & A Home Improvement has helped New Jersey homeowners build spaces they're proud of.

Whether you're starting fresh or restoring what you have, we bring the same attention to detail to every single job.

01

Hardwood Installation

The floor that changes everything.

There's a reason hardwood never goes out of style. It adds warmth, character, and real value to your home, and when it's installed correctly, it lasts a lifetime.

We assess your subfloor, help you choose the right species and finish for your lifestyle, and install with the precision that makes the difference between a floor that looks good and one that feels like home.

The right foundation changes how you experience every room.

02

Sanding & Refinishing

Your floors have more life in them than you think.

Before you replace, let us take a look. Most hardwood floors can be fully restored through professional sanding and refinishing, often at a fraction of the cost of new installation.

We use a dustless sanding process that brings tired, scratched, or worn floors back to their original beauty without the mess, without the disruption, and without losing what makes your floors unique.

You'd be surprised what's hiding under years of wear.

03

Vinyl Installation

Beauty that works as hard as your family does.

Luxury vinyl is the smart choice for busy households. It looks stunning, handles moisture, resists scratches, and stands up to kids, pets, and daily life without missing a beat.

We install luxury vinyl plank and tile in kitchens, bathrooms, basements, and any space where hardwood isn't practical, with the same clean installation and attention to detail we bring to every job.

Durable doesn't have to mean boring.

04

Hardwood Repair

Don't replace what can be saved.

Squeaky boards, water damage, gaps, scratches, or a section that just doesn't look right anymore: we find the problem, fix it cleanly, and match your existing floor so the repair is invisible.

Because the best repair is one nobody notices.

05

Kitchen Remodeling

The heart of your home, reimagined.

Your kitchen should work the way your family lives. We handle the full scope: layout, materials, flooring, and finishing details, with the same craftsmanship that defines every C & A project.

No guesswork. No surprise costs. Just a kitchen you'll love for years.

06

Bathroom Remodeling

A space that feels as good as it looks.

The right bathroom remodel isn't just about aesthetics. It's about function, comfort, and lasting quality. We work with you on tile, flooring, fixtures, and layout to create a space that fits your life and holds up over time.

07

Stairs

The detail most contractors overlook.

Stairs take more daily abuse than almost any surface in your home, and they're one of the first things people notice. We refinish, repair, and install stair treads and risers that are solid, safe, and sharp from top to bottom.

Because the details are what separate good work from great work.
